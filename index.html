Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Here's how it works:

  1. 2 players will input their name and choose a character.
  2. A prompt will appear on screen letting you know whose turn it is.
  3. Click on the square to fill in your spot.
  4. The game will keep track of who wins. The first to 3 wins is declared the winner!
